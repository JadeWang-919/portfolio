NanNan Character Design

Background

Nan Nan is a story-driven, indie horror narrative puzzle game. The story is set in the countryside of northern China in the 1970s.

The story is about the ghost of a 14-year-old girl trying to reveal the mystery of her death. Through exploration and investigation, the players unfolds the story of her life as well as her struggles and mistreatment. With the help of the Five Gods and other supernatural power, will Nan Nan ever be able to escape from her abusive family and find new life?
