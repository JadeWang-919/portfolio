Hi there!

I'm Jade Wang, a passionate UX designer and self-taught illustrator.

Growing up, I have always been very curious about humans. How do people perceive things? How do people behave in different situations? And, how to predict people's behaviors?

This interest in understanding humans led me to study psychology and decision science in college. In the sophomore year, I was introduced to the field of human-computer interaction, where I explored more about the connection between humans and modern technologies. My interest also turned from understanding humans to empathizing with them and creating designs to make life easier and happier.
